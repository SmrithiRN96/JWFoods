About

Our Humble Journey

  • 1973

    The Beginnings

    Advance Food Products Inc., a restaurant distributing supplier was founded by George Trougakos in Scarborough, Ontario

  • 1995

    J & W Foods is Born

    J & W Foods was co-founded in 1995 by Stavros Trougakos & William Fehr, being inspired by their father, George Trougakos. J & W Foods started as a distributor and wholesale/retail butcher shop providing natural premium graded meats.

  • December 2008

    Ontario Inspected Meat Plant

    As the business grew and evolved, their passion and love for high quality meats and foods was reflected more and more in their store. They soon became known as a one-stop-shop for health and nutrition. They have gained a solid reputation for providing high quality Chicken that is air chilled, grain fed, and raised locally in Ontario without the use of hormones and antibiotics, as well as, Beef, Pork and Fish & Seafood, high quality frozen fruits and vegetables, healthy & clean dressings and sauces, supplements & vitamins and sports nutrition products.

  • 2020

    Meal Butler

    Meal Butler was birthed out of a need for high quality cooked & portion controlled meals that can be prepared within minutes. The products are fully cooked, weighed, portioned, and individually flash frozen in BPA free vacuum sealed bags. This service is designed to simplify and help anyone who is interested in creating and/or maintaining a healthy lifestyle. All products are salt and sugar free, with no added oils.

BEEF

It's What's for Dinner

Our flank steak is tender, lean, and rich in flavour — perfect for grilling. It's boneless, comes from below the loin of the cow, and has a strong graining of muscle fibres so it’s best to slice against the grain to ensure steak stays tender. Marinate it to maximize its flavour or simply season it with salt and pepper. Either way, you’re in for a delicious bite. Sourced from the rich cattle land of Alberta, you get superior quality every time • Angus beef • Free of processed and refined ingredients

POULTRY

The chicken everyone loves

Juicy and tender, our whole air chilled chicken is perfect for the barbecue rotisserie or roasted in the oven. Season with your favourite spice blend and a squeeze of fresh lemon juice for the perfect family dinner. Pair with our chef-curated Fingerling Potatoes, caramelized onions and a fresh, crisp salad • Free-run • Vegetable-based grain fed

FISH

Catch A Fish As Fresh As This

With its medium-firm texture, large flakes and mild flavour, our organic fishes are perfect for the grill or skillet. Pair it with a side of basmati rice or roasted smashed potatoes and grilled vegetables for an easy, wholesome dinner any night of the week. It’s also excellent cooked, then chilled and served on salad • Organic • Free of processed or refined ingredients

LAMB

It's a taste of nature

These lamb chops are ideal for grilling and roasting but even better slow cooked for added tenderness and succulence. For fall-off-the-bone results, try pan-searing and then simmering them until done or braise them in tomatoes, red wine, garlic and your favourite seasonings. Perfect with a side of mashed potatoes, or broad egg noodles to soak up that delicious sauce • Grass fed • Certified Halal • Free of processed or refined ingredients

PORK

The Other White Meat

These centre-cut loin chops might be lean but they are packed with flavour! Considered the Cadillac of pork chops, they’ve been aged for tenderness and are so versatile, you can pair any side with them. Season with spices or marinate to your liking and slow cook for ultimate indulgence. The best part is that they stay juicy inside while the outside becomes crispy • Free of processed and refined ingredients

GAME MEAT

A Slice of Growth Straight to Your Plate

Our game meats are naturally lean and tender cut of meats, perfect for grilling or roasting. With its beef-like flavour, it’s what you need for your next barbecue with friends. Toss it on the grill and cook it to medium rare for incredibly delicious and succulent results. Pair it with our chef-curated Lemon Orzo Salad with Feta Cheese for a protein-rich meal that’s on the lighter side. • Raised without antibiotics or added hormones • Grade A • 100% ranch-raised by Canadian farmers
